**Analytics & Reporting Framework for IMIC**

**1\. Policy Analytics**

*   **Policy Applications Overview**
    *   **Total policies submitted**
    *   **Approved vs. rejected applications**
    *   **Rejection reasons (age, invalid reports, payment issues)**
*   **Policy Cover Distribution**
    *   **Average policy cover amount**
    *   **Premium ranges by age group**
    *   **Dependents per policy (avg. family size)**
*   **Agent Performance**
    *   **Policies submitted per agent**
    *   **Approval rate per agent**
    *   **Average processing time per application**

**2\. Claims Analytics**

*   **Claims Volume**
    *   **Total claims submitted**
    *   **Accepted vs. rejected claims**
    *   **Claim rejection reasons (invalid bills, coverage mismatch, fraud detection)**
*   **Claim Settlement**
    *   **Average claim amount**
    *   **Average time to settlement**
    *   **Cheque disbursement tracking**
*   **Member-Level Analysis**
    *   **Claims by primary vs. dependent members**
    *   **High-frequency claimers**
    *   **Claims by hospital/diagnostic center**

**3\. Customer Insights**

*   **Demographics**
    *   **Age distribution of policy holders**
    *   **Geographic distribution (city/state)**
    *   **Family size trends**
*   **Engagement**
    *   **Login frequency of policy holders**
    *   **FAQs usage & site navigation analytics**
*   **Retention**
    *   **Renewal rates**
    *   **Policy lapse/cancellation statistics**

**4\. Operational & Compliance Reports**

*   **System Security**
    *   **Locked accounts due to failed login attempts**
    *   **Manual re-activation logs**
*   **Performance**
    *   **Average response time of system pages**
    *   **Navigation efficiency (clicks to reach key pages)**
*   **Audit Trail**
    *   **Document submission logs**
    *   **Policy approval/rejection decision trail**
    *   **Claim settlement audit trail**

| Dashboard | Stakeholder | Key Metrics |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Policy Dashboard | Insurance Company | Applications, approvals, rejection reasons, premium distribution |
| Claims Dashboard | Insurance Company & Policy Holder | Claim status, settlement time, rejection reasons |
| Agent Dashboard | Agents | Policies submitted, approval rate, claims processed |
| Customer Dashboard | Policy Holder | Policy details, claim history, renewal reminders |
| Compliance Dashboard | Company Auditors | Security logs, SLA breaches, audit trails |
